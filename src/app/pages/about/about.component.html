About Rishikesh Gateway

Your trusted partner for unforgettable adventures in the spiritual capital of India

Our Story

Founded in 2010, Rishikesh Gateway has been at the forefront of adventure tourism in the Himalayas. What started as a small local operation has grown into one of the most trusted travel companies in Uttarakhand.

Our mission is to provide authentic, safe, and memorable experiences that showcase the natural beauty, spiritual significance, and adventure opportunities that Rishikesh has to offer. We believe in sustainable tourism that benefits both travelers and the local community.

Over the years, we have served thousands of travelers from around the world, helping them discover the magic of Rishikesh through river rafting, camping, yoga retreats, and spiritual experiences.
